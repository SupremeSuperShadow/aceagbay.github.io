Power Puzzle

A short first-person 3D puzzle game focused on spatial reasoning.

Controls

  • WASD — Movement
  • Space — Jump
  • Mouse — Look
  • Left Click — Interact

Overview

This project was made to display knowledge of 3D player control, camera handling, 3D graphics, signals, spatial reasoning, and level and sound design.

Tools

  • Godot v4.5.1
  • Sketchpad
  • Audacity
  • BeepBox
