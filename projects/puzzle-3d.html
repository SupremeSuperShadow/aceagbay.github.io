3D Puzzle Room

A short first-person 3D puzzle experience focused on spatial reasoning.

Controls

  • WASD — Movement
  • Mouse — Look / Interact
  • Keyboard — Puzzle interactions

Overview

This project was built to explore 3D player control, camera handling, and puzzle-driven level design within a contained environment.

Problems Solved

  • Designing intuitive 3D player and camera controls
  • Managing puzzle state and progression
  • Ensuring consistent interaction feedback

Systems & Architecture

The game is structured around modular systems for player control, puzzle logic, and environment interaction. Puzzle elements communicate through defined interfaces rather than direct dependencies.

Challenges & Trade-offs

One challenge was balancing camera freedom with player comfort. I chose a constrained camera system to reduce disorientation while maintaining responsiveness.

What I’d Improve Next

  • Expand puzzle variety and complexity
  • Improve visual feedback for interactable objects
  • Refine camera smoothing and transitions
