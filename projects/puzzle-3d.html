Power Puzzle

A short first-person 3D puzzle game focused on spatial reasoning.

Controls

  • WASD — Movement
  • Space — Jump
  • Mouse — Look
  • Left Click — Interact

Tools

  • Godot v4.5.1
  • Sketchpad
  • Audacity
  • BeepBox

Contribution

  • Sole developer

Highlights

  • Custom outline shader for highlighting interactable objects
  • Raycast-based interaction system driving visual feedback
  • Spatial puzzle design with power transfer and platform sequencing
  • Soft-lock prevention and automatic level recovery
